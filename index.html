Regular Cleaning Won't Fix This. Here's Why.

After construction, your space isn't just "a little dusty". Fine silica dust settles in AC vents. Paint splatter bonds to tiles. Cement haze clouds windows. Adhesive residue hides on fixtures. Most cleaners miss these — and you breathe the rest.

  • dangerousFine dust in AC ducts, light fixtures & hidden corners
  • dangerousPaint, cement & adhesive residue on tiles, glass, switches
  • dangerousDebris in ceiling voids, under sinks, behind cabinets
  • dangerousGrout haze, silicone smears, protective film residue
  • dangerousUnvacuumed electrical boxes, vent covers, track systems

Our Difference:

We use HEPA-grade equipment + specialized solvents to remove construction residue without damaging new surfaces. Final walkthrough with you — pay only after approval.
