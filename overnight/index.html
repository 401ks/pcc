Overnight Post-Construction Cleaning: FAQs

What qualifies as an overnight job? expand_more
Any post-construction cleanup needed outside regular hours (evening/night/early morning) with a next-morning or flexible deadline. We assess feasibility case-by-case.
How quickly can I book overnight service? expand_more
Same-day overnight booking is possible. We scale crews to meet your timeline — but unrealistic deadlines (e.g., 3AM booking for 7AM completion) may not be feasible. We'll always propose the fastest viable solution.
What's the overnight premium? expand_more
+25% emergency base +15% overnight surcharge = ~+40% total on standard quote. Distance-based callout fee applies. Transparent, disclosed before work begins.
How does the deposit work for overnight? expand_more
LKR 45,000+ minimum deposit (acts as part-payment, not separate fee). Bank transfer recommended. Balance payable morning of completion (before crew leaves recommended). Unused portion refunded when invoice cleared.

We do a morning walkthrough with you. If you spot a missed area before crew leaves, we re-clean it immediately — free. Guarantee applies to misses identified during walkthrough only.

Entire Colombo district + Kalutara, Panadura, Moragahena, Millewa. Transport charges may apply beyond immediate zone.
