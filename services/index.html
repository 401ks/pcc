Post-Construction Cleaning Services: FAQs

What's the difference between Standard, Emergency, and Overnight? expand_more
Timeline and premium. Standard: 48-hour turnaround, no rush premium. Emergency: 2-hour dispatch, +25% premium. Overnight: Next-morning ready, +40% total premium. Same crews, same guarantee.
Do I need a site visit for a quote? expand_more
Yes. All variants require on-site assessment for accurate pricing. Callout fee: LKR 4,500–6,500 within 5km of Mt. Lavinia (waived for builder partners/returning clients).
How do I choose the right variant? expand_more
Consider your deadline. Need it in 2 hours? Emergency. Ready by morning? Overnight. Flexible 48hrs? Standard. Complex project? Custom. Not sure? WhatsApp us for guidance.
What areas do you serve? expand_more
Entire Colombo district + Kalutara, Panadura, Moragahena, Millewa. Transport charges may apply beyond immediate zone. All variants available across coverage area.
Is the guarantee the same for all variants? expand_more
Yes. Pay after inspection. Re-clean free if noted before crew leaves. Guarantee applies to misses identified during final walkthrough only — for fairness to our crews.
Do you offer builder partnerships? expand_more
Yes. Builder Partnership Program (under development) includes: waived callout fees, dedicated account manager, recurring project discounts. WhatsApp "PARTNER" for early access.
